Jean-Claude Van Damme is a Belgian actor and martial artist best known for his martial arts action films. The most successful of these films include Bloodsport (1988) and Kickboxer (1989).

Timeline of Jean-Claude Van Damme's film career:

  • No Retreat, No Surrender (1986)
  • Bloodsport (1988)
  • Black Eagle (1988)
  • Kickboxer (1989)
  • Universal Soldier(1992)
  • Hard Target (1993)
  • Timecop (1994)
  • Street Fighter(1994)
  • JCVD (2008)
  • The Expendables 2 (2012)
